About app

Simple web application that allows to monitor progress of loans. Every customer has his own process, with hundreds of clients we can easy forget some information e.g. what status that customer have or what offer from bank he got. Trello has great functions but for me that was not enough. With this app we can track customers, add comments, calculate loans or generate report, not only for us but also for brokers who works with our company.
